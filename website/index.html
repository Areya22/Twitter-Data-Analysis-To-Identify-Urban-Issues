ABOUT US

Twitterati is an information, self-voluntary portal that updates locally faced urban problems by the citizens. It is a citizens run service where the data is collected from Twitter and used to analyze and publicize issues faced in urban regions of India. Currently it's main focus is Mumbai, but we plan to make it a pan-India service.

Mission: Inform the people of the problems being faced and keep them updated about the actions being taken on a fortnightly basis.
